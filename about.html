Talia Malfassi
I'm a traveller in the creative
world of design.
I grew up in the theater world and learned how to communicate with people by designing space. My journey brought me to the design of everyday life places. I used every graphic design tool I know to reach people. I played with shapes and colours, paper and printed matter, as well as digital formats and moving images. I learned to observe others and understand their way of thinking. Curiosity and observation guide my process, from concept to production. My bag is always ready for the next visual journey.
