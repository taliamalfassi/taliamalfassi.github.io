Carbrando

BRAND STRATEGY UX & UI DESIGN

The best way to find your new used car.
The car market is changing and those who work in the automotive sector must evolve together with it. Buying a new car becomes more and more complex due to costs and long waiting times caused by the lack of production materials. The solution lies in used cars.
DATE

December 2021

ROLE

Brand Strategist
UX / UI Designer
Graphic Designer

COMPANY

Brandini S.p.a.

CHALLENGE

It is difficult for a person to make an expensive purchase on the Internet.
It is even more difficult when it comes to used things, especially if it is a car.

USER RESEARCH

Through a campaign on social media, a sample of people was selected to be interviewed to find the pain points
in the purchase of a used car, with a particular focus on the web.
In exchange for the collaboration, they received discount coupons for the purchase of a used car.

PAIN POINTS
1
LACK OF CONFIDENCE
2
LACK OF INFORMATION
3
DIFFICULTY IN SEARCHING
4
NEED FOR EXPERT SUPPORT
VISUAL DESIGN SOLUTION
SOLUTION

A new corporate image that breaks away from the historical name of the company. A new name for a new reality with modern sales processes.

SOLUTION

The choice of playful graphics is intended to be more familiar and close to everyone. The colors also want to give a new and fresher tone to the company.

UX SOLUTION

The approach of this new reality will be aimed more at providing purchase support
rather than trying to offer offers and sell cars. Carbrando must be a helpful tool for the user.

SOLUTION

The homepage is clean and clear, with no invasive advertising.

Immediately the user can request help from a consultant.

SOLUTION

the search tools are designed to help the user find the right car for him faster and more accurately

SOLUTION

On the vehicle page, it is possible to see the car in all its details thanks to the large gallery and the possibility of viewing the car at 360°.

The price is also clearly visible to express clarity and honesty.

HIGH FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

The site prototype was created both in the desktop and mobile version, prioritizing the
needs of the user that change according to the device they use.
