Il Circuito della Stostenibilità

NAMING VISUAL DESIGN

Enegan feat Brandini.
Enegan is a manufacturer and supplier of clean energy, and
Brandini is a historic Tuscan car dealer group. These two
realities have joined forces to promote their products and
services related to green mobility.
DATE

September 2020

ROLE

Visual Designer

COMPANY

Brandini Spa
Enegan Spa

APPROACH

The name of the campaign sets off the collaboration of the two companies,
underlining that one supports the other in a vicious circle.
A play on words is also created: “circuit” is both a symbol
of the union of the two realities and a reference to the car racetrack.
