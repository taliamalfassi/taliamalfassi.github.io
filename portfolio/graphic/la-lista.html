LA LISTA?

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Eco Campaign
A persistent campaign has been carried out throughout
the Tuscan territory by launching a strong message.
the tools used to convey communication were both digital
and traditional advertising.
DATE

March 2019

ROLE

Graphic Designer

COMPANY

Brandini Spa

APPROACH

"Do you have a list?".
This question should arouse in people a feeling of inadequacy and therefore urgency in wanting to fill it.
The graphics, on the other hand, are linked to the other message present to underline the themes: ecology and savings.
The fact that there are no other information fuels the curiosity of the viewer.
