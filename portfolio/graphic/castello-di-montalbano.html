Castello di Montalbano

BRAND IDENTITY

A place for romance and relaxation.
Castello di Montalbano is a hotel in the hills near Florence.
A unique location with ancient roots where couples can
find a magical place to spend their love escape.
DATE

November 2020

ROLE

Visual Designer

COMPANY

Domina Service sas

APPROACH

The branding required an ancient but delicate taste to describe not only the history of the place but also the sensations one experiences as its guest. This is the origin of the choice of a serif whose austerity is attenuated by the alternation of pastel and warm colors.

PICTOGRAM

The symbol synthesises the facade of the castle, transforming itself into the “H” of the hotel.

LOGO & TYPEFACES

For the logo, I chose the Mermaid Bold typeface. It looks ancient but it is not old and it has its personality. For the geographical indication, on the other hand, I chose to use the Calibri Regular, which is clearer and more aseptic as it is. I used the same font for the rest of the communications.
