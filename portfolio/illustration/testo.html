Testone!

ILLUSTRATION EDITORIAL

Testo, come si diventa un libro
Testone! is the daily born to tell about Testo - the publishing industry festival- at the Stazione Leopolda in Florence.
The editorial staff has worked for three days to keep track of events, writers and other guests. I took part in the work as an illustrator, I dealt with interviews with the designers working in the editorial field, I followed the print phase of the newspaper, and naturally, I created the illustrations that accompanied the articles.

APPROACH

The daily was printed using the Risograph method. It was crucial to keep in mind the characteristics of this type of print to transform its limitations into potentiality. Hence, the choice to use bold and rough lines.
