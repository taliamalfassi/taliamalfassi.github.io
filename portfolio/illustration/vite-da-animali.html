Vite da Animali

ILLUSTRATION EDITORIAL

A silent story
This is a collection of stories told in pictures.
The main characters are humanized animals who live out of time, along with their little pains and little joys.

APPROACH

Every photo tells you something. You don't know what happened when the picture was taken and you don't know the subjects' story and their relationship, but you can perceive little signs. Many starting points can arise from these cues. I gather old pictures from the thrift shop and then I build a story out of them. The aesthetics are intended as a tribute to cinematic neorealism.
