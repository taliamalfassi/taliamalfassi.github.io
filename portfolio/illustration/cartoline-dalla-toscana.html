Cartoline dalla Toscana

ILLUSTRATION PUBLISHING

Il trippaio di Porta Romana
One of my works was selected in the contest organized by Calimaia Studio to become a postcard
The call for bids asked us to show a glimpse of Tuscany through a work of art. The postcard collection included 24 pieces created with a limited edition Risograph print. The exhibition took place in Florence at EX FO and in Lido di Camaiore at Bagno Patrizia.

Saluti dalla Toscana

APPROACH

For me, home is my neighborhood, with its colorful characters and artisans' shops. One of these shops is the food truck around the corner of the Art Institute's garden, selling "lampredotto" and other traditional florentine foods.
This illustration is an homage to the neighborhood that has embraced me from the first day I moved to Tuscany, making me feel at home.
